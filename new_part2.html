The new part 2

For a new thing to appear it had to come in replacement of an old one. In tech terms the beta version aka the 2.0 comes as an upgrade to the alpha version aka the 1.0. So it is with many things in this world. The single woman version has to die to be a married woman, the girl has to die to give room for the mother in her to be born, the child version of you give way to the adult. And so on.... Even more so with the Lord Jesus, He had to abandon His glory in heaven to be a man to die for our sins, and in recieving Him then we become like Him. So how then do we get the courage to transition, by fixing our gaze on the His word and holding on to the truth that He never forgets what we do for His name. Jesus in Mathew 10:42, said that even giving a cup of cold water to a little one is not forgotten... So I charge you today, be fully pursuaded in whom you have believed and why you do what you do name. #byHisgraceforHisglory
